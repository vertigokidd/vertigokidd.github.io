Life of a Keurig

What happens when you monitor the electricity consumption of an office coffee maker.

About

I monitored the electricity usage of an office Keurig machine over the course of two weeks in May/June 2016. The hardware included a hacked wattage meter and a Raspberry Pi, which streamed data to AWS every two seconds. That data was collected, processed, and stored completely in the AWS cloud using Kinesis, S3, Lambda, and EC2.


I'm a data engineer specializing in Hadoop and AWS.
